Critical Project Milestones

Month 1 - Week 2
Project Kickoff & Stakeholder Alignment

All key stakeholders engaged, roles defined, communication protocols established

Month 2 - Week 4
Program Design Approval

Complete program framework approved by executive sponsor and wellness committee

Month 3 - Week 2
Technology Platform Selection

Step tracking platform selected, vendor contracts signed, technical specifications finalized

Month 4 - Week 3
Vendor Partnerships Established

Food vendor agreements in place, mental health resources secured, space modifications complete

Month 5 - Week 2
Pilot Program Launch

Limited rollout with 50 employees across departments, initial feedback collection begins

Month 5 - Week 4
Pilot Results & Go/No-Go Decision

Pilot evaluation complete, adjustments identified, executive approval for full implementation

Month 6 - Week 1
Organization-wide Launch

All employees invited to participate, training sessions begin, support systems active

Month 7 - Week 4
Full Implementation Complete

All program components operational, 75% employee participation target achieved

Month 8 - Week 4
Initial Results Assessment

First comprehensive evaluation of health outcomes, participation rates, and ROI metrics

Resource Allocation by Phase

Planning Phase

Resources: Project Manager (100%), Wellness Committee (50%), Executive Team (25%)

Budget: $15,000 (12% of total)

Setup Phase

Resources: IT Team (75%), Facilities (100%), Vendors (100%)

Budget: $60,000 (48% of total)

Pilot Phase

Resources: All Teams (50%), Pilot Participants (50 employees)

Budget: $10,000 (8% of total)

Implementation

Resources: All Teams (100%), All Employees (participation)

Budget: $25,000 (20% of total)

Evaluation

Resources: Analytics Team (100%), Project Manager (75%)

Budget: $15,000 (12% of total)

Timeline Risk Factors & Mitigation

High Risk: Vendor Delays

Impact: 2-4 week delay in setup phase

Mitigation: Multiple vendor options, early contracting, penalty clauses

Medium Risk: Technical Issues

Impact: 1-2 week delay in pilot phase

Mitigation: Extended testing period, backup systems, IT support

Medium Risk: Low Pilot Participation

Impact: 2-3 week delay in implementation

Mitigation: Incentive programs, management support, communication

Low Risk: Space Modifications

Impact: 1 week delay in setup

Mitigation: Early planning, alternative locations, phased approach
